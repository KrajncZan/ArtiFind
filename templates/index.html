How does ArtiFind work?
ArtiFind combines online search, interactive maps, and a social network to unveil hidden artworks in cities and nature.
Who can use ArtiFind?
Art lovers, artists, and adventurous tourists seeking unique art experiences in new places can all benefit from ArtiFind.
What technologies power ArtiFind?
ArtiFind is a tech marvel using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL to create a seamless art exploration experience for users.
Why choose ArtiFind?
ArtiFind connects art with the environment, allowing you to discover and share art in unexpected places anytime, anywhere.
Can I contribute to ArtiFind?
Absolutely! Artists and users can share their discovered or created artworks, interact, rate art, and build a vibrant art community on ArtiFind.
